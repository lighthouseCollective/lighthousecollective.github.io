Fast Park

  • BRANDING & WEB DESIGN

  • 2020

Fast Park is a mobile application solution for state and national parks. The color palette was chosen to align with the National Park arrowhead badge. The iconic logo is meant to highlight an F and a P while creating a graphic that alludes to entering and exiting.

The website was designed to enable customers to quickly and easily download the app, while highlighting a few of the many useful features of Fast Park.

New England Family First Life

  • WEB DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

  • NOV 2019

The site revolves around a linked NavBar that follows the user down the page. After one initial load, all website content is available throughout the vertical page, preventing undesirable loading time. While the site is vertically dense, it is visually stimulating to counteract the short attention spans of modern consumers.

At any time, the viewer can return to the top of the page by clicking the take a look around button on the top left of the screen. Because the NavBar follows the user, it is very easy and quick to navigate to the varying sections of the site.
